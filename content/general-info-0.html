The 90 acre Greater Gulf State Fairgrounds are located in West Mobile at the intersection of Cody Road and Zeigler Boulevard. The Fairgrounds are available for almost every type of event imaginable, large or small, indoors or outdoors. It is completely fenced for privacy and control with paved parking for 1,000 vehicles, and total parking for up to 5,000 vehicles. A covered drive allows protected loading and unloading of passengers to the Hocklander Hall and Grand Ballroom.

The Fair staff is available to answer any questions and to help plan your event. Call (251) 344-4573 for more information and a video of the facilities.

New events must fill out an event application.
