directions


1035 Cody Road North – Mobile, AL 36608 – (251) 344-4573

From I-65 North/South

  • Take Exit 5A, Springhill Ave. W.
  • Proceed 5.3 miles on Springhill Ave. (after 2 miles, Springhill Ave turns into Zeigler Blvd)
  • Fairgrounds are on the right, immediately past Cody Rd.

From I-10 East/West

  • Take Exit 13, Theodore-Dawes Rd
  • Follow signs to Mobile Airport
  • Theodore-Dawes Rd becomes Schillinger Rd.
  • Proceed to Zeiglar Blvd (approx. 10.9 miles)
  • Turn right and proceed 1 mile to the Fairgrounds on the left
