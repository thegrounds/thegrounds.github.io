Hours/Admissions/Discounts

Welcome to one of the top State Fairs in North America, showcasing our state's agriculture, manufacturing and commerce. It's everything from family entertainment and educational exhibits to exciting competitions and just plain fun!

Hours

Gates open at 8 a.m. daily.

Buildings

Sunday - Thursday: 10 a.m. to 9 p.m.

Friday - Saturday: 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.

Outside Vendors

Sunday - Thursday: 10 a.m. to 9:30 p.m.

Friday - Saturday: 10 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.

Carnival

Monday: Opens at noon

Tuesday - Friday: Opens at 1 p.m.

Saturday - Sunday: Opens at 11 a.m.

Closing times are at the discretion of management.

Gate Admission

Adults (ages 12+): $10

Children (ages 6-11): $5

Children (ages 5 and under): FREE!

Season Passes: $30

Advance Gate Admission Deals

Discount Outside Gate Admission Tickets are available at the The Grandstand Box Office, online at okstatefair.com or by calling 405.948.6800 starting July 8 thru September 13, 2017, only.

Adults (ages 12+): $7

Children (ages 6-11): $4

Discount Gate Admission Tickets will also be available starting Monday, August 14, at OnCue Convenience Stores, MidFirst Banking Centers (cash only), Cox Solutions Stores and Walgreens in the Oklahoma City Metro Area.

Special Discount Days

Thursday, September 14: Opening Day - $2

Monday, September 18: School Kids' Day – FREE admission for all kids thru grade 12

Tuesday, September 19: Super Saver Tuesday - $3, Armed Forces' Day - FREE admission for Armed Forces personnel and spouses (Military I.D. required)

Wednesday, September 20: Senior Citizens' Day – FREE admission for Senior Citizens 55+ (I.D. required)

Rentals

Lockers, Wheelchairs, Electric Scooters, Strollers and Wagons are available for rent just inside three main gates of the The Grounds (near the The Grandstand just inside Gate 6, on the north side of 14 Flags Plaza near Gate 2 and just inside Gate 9 near the Petting Zoo). Rentals are available for walk-up customers and also by advance reservation. Fairgoers can make reservations for scooters and wheelchairs online at www.vistamobility.net or by calling 877.588.4782.

Rentals are provided by a third party contractor and not The Grounds, Inc. Prices and information are subject to change without notice.
