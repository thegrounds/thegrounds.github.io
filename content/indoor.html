Hocklander Hall

Hocklander Hall lends itself admirably to trade shows, flea markets, concerts, and sporting events. Roll-up doors provide easy access for unloading and are large enough to accommodate recreational vehicles, trailers, boats, trucks and cars.

• 360′ x 120′ – 43,200 square feet

• Concrete Floor

• Restrooms

• Office Space

• 14′ high x 16′ wide overhead door

• 400 amp three phase power (east end of building)

Ballroom / The Grand Ballroom

• 177′ x 120′ – 21,240 square feet
• Part carpeted, part tiled floor
• PA system
• Restrooms across the hallway
• 14′ high x 20′ wide Overhead Door
• Power: two services of 200 amp three phase
• Climate controlled

Capacities of the Grand Ballroom
• 1,800 Banquet (tables and chairs)

In addition, the Grand Ballroom can be divided into two smaller rooms by a collapsible dividing wall. The tiled side is the EXHIBIT HALL and the carpeted side is the BALLROOM. Both sides together are called the GRAND BALLROOM. It adjoins and can be used in conjunction with Hocklander Hall.

Exhibit Hall – (Tiled side)
• 100′ x 120′
• 12,000 square feet
• Climate controlled

Capacities of Exhibit Hall
• 800 Banquet (tables and chairs)

Ballroom (carpeted side of Grand Ballroom)
• 77′ x 120′
• 9,240 square feet
• Climate controlled

Capacities of Ballroom
