The Grandstand

The Grandstand @ The Grounds is perfect for any type of musical event from blues festivals to rock acts to a country hoedown. The large field in front of the stage can accommodate an audience of up to 20,000 people. The stage can also be used for speeches, announcements, etc.

Features

40′ x 40′ covered stage, 4′ high
23′x32′ covered sound wings
Dressing Rooms
Power available on stage: 400 amp three phase disconnect, two services of 200 amp disconnects, 220 amp breaker panel, additional 200 amp disconnect behind stage.
