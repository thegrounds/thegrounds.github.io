Family Land

This family-focused area provides entertainment activities for children of all ages including 30 kid friendly rides, family games, and Noah’s Way Exotic Petting Zoo!

For information regarding ride height requirements be sure to visit the Size Wise stations located next to each ride. Please be advised that children under 42” may be required to ride some of the rides with a paying adult.
