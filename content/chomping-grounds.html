the chomping grounds


Featuring over 30,000 sq feet of seated dining space the Chomping Grounds is the perfect place to relax and enjoy some tasty fair food. In addition to everyone’s Fair favorites like chicken-on-a-stick and funnel cakes, the Chomping Grounds will also feature amazing local food vendors like The Hungry Owl, Bamboo Hibachi and Sushi, La Cocina, and many other Gulf Coast staples!
