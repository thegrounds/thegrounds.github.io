About


The Grounds is a year-round entertainment, convention and commercial venue home to several of the Gulf Coast’s largest events. With more than one million annual attendees to our campus, we are a leader in the area of community development.


In 2013, The Grounds, formerly known as the Greater Gulf State Fairgrounds, underwent a major restructuring and rebranding positioning the facility as one of the premiere event and entertainment spaces along the Gulf Coast. Along with the facility’s $300,000 facelift, the Greater Gulf State Fair (GGSF) was also reorganized with an enhanced focus on safety, family fun, and one-of-a-kind attractions for guests of all ages. The activity on The Grounds is not limited to the 10 days of the annual GGSF. We host events of all sizes, year- round. The Grounds has the opportunity to host everything from fellow non-profits and their respective events to concerts, balls, and community service projects. In addition, The Grounds and our board, staff, and volunteers plan and implement numerous events from color runs to various agricultural shows.

Our 100-acre property is located in West Mobile at the intersection of Cody Road and Zeigler Boulevard. The indoor and outdoor facilities are available for almost every type of event imaginable no matter how large or small. The property is completely fenced for privacy and access control with paved parking for 1,000 vehicles and total parking for up to 5,000 vehicles. A covered drive allows protected loading and unloading of passengers to Grounds Central and the Grand Ballroom.
The Grounds also has the capacity to host 200+ campers/RVs via camper/RV individual hook ups around the campus. We can also provide production/sound/stage equipment, catering, security, sanitation/clean up, and advertising via The Grounds’ contractors if needed.

The Fair

The Greater Gulf State fair

It’s time for the Fair! The Grounds will host the 62nd annual Greater Gulf State Fair from October 28 – November 6, 2016. This year’s Fair will feature a new theme “Fair Takes Flight” which aims to host feature the greatness of everything flight related under one roof as well as more than 30 adult rides, 30 kid rides and a must-see entertainment line-up!
